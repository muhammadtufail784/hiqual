Data Protection

Data Protection at Hiqual UK

At Hiqual UK, we recognize the importance of protecting the personal information of the individuals we interact with, including current and prospective employees, Learning Partners, learners, suppliers, clients, and others. We are committed to complying with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and the UK General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (Data Protection Law) to ensure that personal data is managed lawfully and effectively.

Key Principles:

1. Fair Collection and Use of Information: Hiqual UK ensures that personal information is collected and used fairly, with clear purposes specified for its use.

2. International Data Transfers: Personal information is not transferred to countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) without suitable safeguards in place.

3. Data Security: We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to safeguard personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Individual Rights: Hiqual UK respects the rights of individuals under the Data Protection Law and ensures that these rights are fully exercised.

5. Data Retention: We hold personal information for no longer than necessary, taking into account legal requirements and operational needs.

6. Data Quality: Hiqual UK ensures the accuracy and quality of the personal information we collect, store, and process.

7. Legal Obligations and Operational Needs: We collect and process appropriate information necessary to fulfill our operational needs and comply with legal requirements.

Subject Access Request:

Individuals have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about them. To make a formal request under the Data Protection Law, please submit a subject access request to Hiqual UK. You can contact our Data Protection Representative by emailing info@hiqual.co.uk.
