Privacy Policy

HiQual UK is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your personal information when you use our services. We value the trust you place in us and strive to handle your personal information with care.

Information Collection and Use

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, and professional information when you interact with our services or provide information on our platforms. This information is used for the purpose of delivering our educational services and improving our offerings to meet your needs.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of your personal information. This includes safeguarding data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. We regularly review our security procedures to ensure your data is protected.

Information Sharing

We may share your personal information with trusted partners and service providers who assist us in delivering our educational services. These third parties are obligated to handle your information securely and confidentially.

Compliance with Laws

HiQual UK operates in compliance with applicable data protection laws and regulations. We are committed to upholding your rights and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

Your Choices

You have the right to access, update, and correct your personal information. If you wish to make changes or have any concerns about how your data is being used, please contact us at info@hiqual.co.uk.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or modifications.

For any queries regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@hiqual.co.uk.
